Brand Basics

Brand Identity & Principles

The foundational values and guidelines that define who EZFacility is.

Mission Statement

Our mission is to transform the way our clients attract, manage and engage their customers. We do this by delighting them with our feature-rich management software and fanatical customer service.

Company Name

The official company name is "EZFacility, Inc." — used only for professional and formal documentation. The company is more commonly referred to as "EZFacility", which is how the company should be named in all copy.

Our Story

In 2003, we created EZFacility with a vision to build a great company – one that changes the way businesses are run through cutting edge technology; one that delivers real value to our customers; one that creates substantial rewards and opportunities for our employees; and one that is an enjoyable place to work.

The following guidelines have been developed to ensure the correct usage of EZFacility's brand identity wherever it is reproduced and applied. Do not deviate from the guideline. Any departure from the documentation will be a misrepresentation of EZFacility both as a brand and as a business.

Brand Identity

Logo Usage – Do's & Don'ts

Only approved variations of the logo can be used and cannot be altered in any way.

✓ Correct Usage

  • Use the full color logo whenever possible
  • Use white logo on dark or photographic backgrounds
  • Maintain designated clear space (min. 0.5" around logo)
  • Use only approved color variations
  • Scale proportionally at all times

✗ Incorrect Usage

  • Re-arrange the order of elements
  • Change orientation or rotation
  • Disproportionately scale or resize
  • Change the color of elements
  • Recreate the logo or change the font
  • Add special effects to the logo
  • Display logo as outline text
  • Place on contrasting background
  • Add other elements not specified
  • Crop the logo in any way

Clear Space Rule

Size & Positioning

Whenever possible, isolate the EZFacility logo or icon from other copy or graphic elements by creating a designated amount of clear space around it equal to the distance indicated by "X". The size of "X" should never be smaller than 0.5 inches in width on either side of the logo.

Visual System

Photography Style

Photography should feel authentic, professional, and aligned with the EZFacility brand environment.

✓ Do's – Correct Usage

  • Environment: Fitness facilities, gyms, yoga studios, weight rooms, wellness facilities, spas
  • Lighting: Natural, warm feel or glow
  • Models: Fitness trainers, club owners, wellness staff, coaches, professional business owners (mainly middle-aged males, business professionals)
  • Wardrobe: Fitness or gym attire, casual professional wear — business appropriate but not suit and tie
  • Color Palette: Established brand colors
  • Software: Self-service, point of sale, scheduling software shown on laptops, tablets, multiple devices

✗ Don'ts – Incorrect Usage

  • Environment: Corporate offices, hotels, conference rooms, school classrooms
  • Lighting: Cold, dark and dull colors, weird cropping, no natural light
  • Models: Too young or overly hip fitness models, business attire/suits, overly cheesy smiles, direct eye contact, close-ups, non-believable expressions, all-female audience, religious subjects
  • Wardrobe: Corporate attire, suits, dress shoes, button-ups, high heels, dresses, or overly revealing workout gear
  • No nudity
